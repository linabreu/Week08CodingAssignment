Cities In Arizona

The capital city of Arizona, Phoenix, is ranked as the 5th most populated city in the United States with a population of 1.6 million people.

Arizona is home to several other large cities, most notably Glendale, which hosted the 2023 Superbowl and was the first stop on Taylor Swift's Eras Tour.

Also noteable city is Buckeye, Arizona, which was recently named one of the nation's fastest growing cities.

Arizona has three public universities, Arizona State University, University of Arizona and Northern Arizona University located in Tempe, Tucson and Flagstaff respectively.
